Why EasyRead?

Easy read is an AI-powered tool utilizes advanced natural language processing algorithms to summarize complex text into concise, easy-to-understand summaries. In addition, it can generate practice tests and study guides based on the text, allowing users to reinforce their knowledge and prepare for exams efficiently.

How to use EasyRead

Step 1 - Login

Click on the login button in the top right and follow the steps. If you do not wish to log in you may continue as a guest.

Step 2 - Upload Your Article

After Logging In, you will be redirected to a page where you can upload your article. Copy your article from the source and paste it into the box provided.

Step 3 - Select Your Task

After uploading your article, select the type of help that you would like to recieve. After selecting your task you should get your results in under 2 minutes.
